Why would evaluation of biomechanics be important for sports players or athletes?

  • Better understanding of body impacts on mechanics and movements during training and competition.
  • Detection of any potential risk of sport injury.
  • Detection of dysfunctional areas that are painful which require treatments.
  • Improving posture, muscle balance, and fundamental mobility in physical activities to maintain a healthy spine.
  • Optimizing overall function of the body.
